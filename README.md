## What is Flow Music

Flow Music is a browser-based music generation platform that uses AI to create original songs, instrumental tracks, background music, and audio content from text descriptions, genre tags, or melodic references. It is designed for content creators, musicians, podcasters, and anyone who needs custom audio without requiring traditional music production skills.

The platform supports multiple generation modes: text-to-music, lyrics-to-song, style-transfer, and melody continuation. Users can describe the mood, genre, tempo, and instrumentation they want, and Flow Music generates a complete audio track in seconds.

## Why use Flow Music

Traditional music production requires instruments, recording equipment, studio time, and technical expertise. Flow Music removes these barriers by letting anyone generate professional-quality audio from a simple text prompt. Whether you need background music for a video, a jingle for a podcast, or inspiration for a full song, Flow Music delivers results instantly without downloads or setup.

Key advantages:
- No music production experience needed
- Generate full tracks in seconds
- Multiple genres and styles available
- Browser-based, no software installation
- Export in standard audio formats

## How Flow Music works

1. **Describe your sound**: Enter a text description of the music you want to create, including genre, mood, tempo, and instrumentation.
2. **Choose generation mode**: Select text-to-music, lyrics-to-song, or style transfer depending on your input type.
3. **Generate and download**: The AI processes your request and produces a complete audio track. Preview, tweak parameters, or download directly.
