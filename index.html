Workflows

How Flow Music fits real music production workflows

The strongest use of Flow Music is not just "generate a track." It is about speeding up composition, testing musical ideas, and producing custom audio for projects without needing a full studio setup.

  1. Start with a text description or lyrics. Use descriptive genre, mood, tempo, and instrumentation language instead of vague terms.
  2. Choose the right generation path. Text-to-music is better for ideation and exploring genres. Lyrics-to-song is better when you have written lyrics and want a full composition.
  3. Review, refine, and export. Use the official showcase and output examples to calibrate style before you generate final production tracks.
